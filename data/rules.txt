- In the dice game there are two dice, one belonging to each player.
- Each player takes turns to roll their dice, once you receive a roll,
different points will be added and subtracted depending on its nature.
- If a player rolls an even number, 10 points are added to their score.
- If a player rolls an odd number, then 5 points are subtracted from their score,
unless it would cause their score to become negative.
- If a player rolls a double, they receive an additional roll.
- There are five rounds, where each rounds of each player rolling their dice once.
- If both players scores are the same at the end of the five rounds,
then additional rounds will commence until the scores are no longer the same.
